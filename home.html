OUR HISTORY

History I – The Beginning

“Silog” comes from sinangag and itlog — a simple Filipino meal that became a symbol of comfort and satisfaction.

History II – Growing Popularity

Tapsilog inspired countless variations such as longsilog, tocilog, bangsilog, and hotsilog.

History III – Community Favorite

Silogans flourished near schools, terminals, and workplaces, especially for night-shift workers.

History IV – Night Shift Silogan'22

Night Shift Silogan'22 continues this tradition with quality meals, warm service, and a welcoming vibe.

LIFE OF THE OWNER

The owner of Night Shift Silogan’22 started with a simple dream—to provide honest, comforting Filipino meals for hardworking people, especially those on night shifts. Through dedication, long hours, and a passion for cooking, the business grew from a small idea into a place where customers feel at home. Every challenge became motivation, and every customer became part of the journey.

SUCCESS STORY OF THE BUSINESS

Night Shift Silogan’22 began as a small idea fueled by hard work and determination. With limited resources but a strong passion for serving quality Filipino comfort food, the business slowly gained the trust of customers. Through consistency, long nights, and dedication to great taste and service, Night Shift Silogan’22 grew into a favorite spot for students, workers, and night-shift heroes. Today, its success is built on perseverance, community support, and a commitment to serve every customer with heart.

CHALLENGES MET BY THE OWNER

Building Night Shift Silogan’22 was not easy. The owner faced challenges such as limited capital, long working hours, and the pressure of running the business day and night. There were moments of slow sales, physical exhaustion, and tough decisions. However, through patience, discipline, and strong determination, each challenge became a lesson that helped the business grow stronger and more stable.

HOW WILL YOU SEE YOUR BUSINESS TEN YEARS FROM NOW

Ten years from now, Night Shift Silogan’22 is envisioned to grow into a well-known and trusted Silogan brand. The business aims to expand to multiple branches while maintaining the same quality, affordability, and warm service that customers love today. More than growth, the goal is to continue serving the community, creating jobs, and becoming a go-to comfort food place for every Filipino, day or night.
